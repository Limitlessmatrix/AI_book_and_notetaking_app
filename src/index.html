How to Use Voice Notes

  1. 1
    Click the big microphone button to start recording.
    Speak naturally — there's no rush.
  2. 2
    Click it again to stop.
    The app will convert your speech to text in a few seconds.
  3. 3
    Read and edit the text in the notes area below.
  4. 4
    Click "Save File" to save your notes to your computer.

💡 Tip: The app works offline after the first setup. No internet needed!
